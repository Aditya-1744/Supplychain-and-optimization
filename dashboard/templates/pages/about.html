Data Processing Pipeline

📊

1. Historical Data

3 years of SKU-level daily demand across 5 warehouses (91,250 rows), plus SKU master with ABC/XYZ classification and cost/price data.

⚙️

2. Feature Engineering

Weekly and annual seasonality via Fourier terms, promo flags, coefficient-of-variation based XYZ classification.

📈

3. Demand Forecast

A production router selects Fourier regression, Gradient Boosting, or Croston per SKU based on archetype and XYZ class.

✅

4. Optimization

(s,S) policy parameters and multi-echelon pooling math generate order-up-to levels honoring warehouse capacity constraints.

📉

Forecasting Engine

Fourier Seasonal Regression + GBM

Fourier regression (Ridge) decomposes demand into trend, weekly, and annual seasonality. Gradient Boosting handles erratic Z-class SKUs. The router picks per-SKU based on ABC/XYZ class and demand CV.

intermittent → Croston / moving-average baseline
smooth + X/Y → Fourier seasonal model
smooth + Z → GBM (disagreement flag)
🧮

Optimization Engine

Multi-Echelon (s, S) Policy

Safety stock via SS = Z × σ × √L. A discrete-event simulation runs (s,S) trajectories over 90-day holdout windows; pooling math quantifies the coordination saving from centralized vs decentralized safety stock.
